## Slide 10 — "It's Already Happening"

**Opening:** "These aren't projections — these are last year's results."

### Card-by-card talking points:

- **Klarna** — Doubled revenue while cutting workforce in half. Customer issues resolved in 2 minutes instead of 11. +$40M profit.

- **Google** — Half of all new code at Google is now written by AI. Was 25% just a year ago. Engineering teams are 10% faster.

- **Microsoft** — $13B in AI revenue growing 175% year-over-year. 85% of their own employees use Copilot every day — they're eating their own cooking.

- **Reddit** — Support response time dropped 84%. Nearly half of all support cases are fully automated now.

- **ING** *(Dutch — relatable for us)* — 90% of their AI experiments make it to production. Industry average is below 30% — some studies say as low as 12%. Their secret: one central AI platform + board-level sponsorship. Also: KYC due diligence went from days/weeks to seconds. Launching AI-assisted mortgages in NL and Germany this year — human advice stays, but AI handles all the paperwork.

- **Booking.com** *(Dutch — relatable for us)* — $700M in transformation savings being reinvested into growth in 2026. Customer service cost per booking dropped 10% while bookings grew 10%. That's a real P&L line item, not a pilot metric. Partner satisfaction up 73% after launching AI messaging tools.

**Transition to next slide:** "So that's individual companies. But what does the market as a whole look like?"
